Squirrels, Ducks, Geese, Fish, At the park, these four species lived in harmony, eating breadcrumbs and chasing children. Until one day, the geese attacked.
Little by little, the geese began to take over the entire park. Until today, the other animals found a doomsday device. This device can end the geese' reign of terror once and for all.
Unfortunately, this device seems to be password protected. Can you crack the code and save the park?
